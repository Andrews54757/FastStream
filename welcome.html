FastStream

FastStream is a fancy video player. Its primary purpose is to get rid of ads and buffering when streaming video.

Usage

There are two ways to use FastStream

  1. You can go to a website with a video stream, and turn faststream on by clicking on the icon. Play the stream and the client will be auto-replaced with faststream
  2. You can go to a new tab, and press the extension icon to go to the player.

Once you got the video player open, hover over the left side of the video to open the sidebar (search/playlist).

Keyboard Controls

  • Left/Right arrow keys - 1 second seek forward/back
  • <> keys - 10 second seek forward/back
  • z key - Undo seek
  • Up/Down arrow keys - 10% volume up/down
  • f - Full screen
  • +- - Add/decrease concurrent requests.
  • ~ - Force retry key - use this to re-attempt failed downloads
  • s - Skip intro/outro if applicable
  • Alt - Hide/show player
